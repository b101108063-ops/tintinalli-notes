Section 05：止痛與麻醉#

疼痛評估 (Pain Assessment)#

  • 75-80% of ED patients present with pain as primary complaint
  • Oligoanalgesia (疼痛治療不足) 普遍存在
  • 高風險族群：種族少數、年老、年幼、認知障礙
  • 疼痛治療不應因擔心成癮而省略

疼痛量表#

Scale Method 特點
VAS 0-100mm linear scale 13mm = 最小顯著改變；30mm = 最小可接受改變
NRS 0-10 numeric 最常用
5-point global 0-4 descriptor 1 point 改變 = 大改變

疼痛程度分級#

  • Mild：VAS 0-30mm, NRS 0-3/4
  • Moderate：VAS 40-60mm, NRS 4-6/7
  • Severe：VAS >60mm, NRS >6/7

鴉片類止痛藥 (Opioid Analgesics)#

First-line Opioids#

Drug Dose (Adult) Onset Duration Notes
Morphine 0.1 mg/kg IV 1-2 min (IV) 1-2h (IV) Histamine release → hypotension/nausea
10 mg IM/SC 15-30 min (IM) 3-4h (IM/SC)
0.3 mg/kg PO 10-15 min -
Hydromorphone 0.015 mg/kg IV 3-5 min 2-4h More euphoria than morphine
1-2 mg IM 7-10 min peak -
Fentanyl 1 mcg/kg IV <1 min 30-60 min Less CV depression; >5 mcg/kg → chest wall rigidity
100 mcg nasal spray - - Breakthrough cancer pain; wait >2h
100 mcg buccal tablet - - Breakthrough cancer pain; wait >4h

Avoid/Use Caution#

Drug Issue
Meperidine Normeperidine accumulation → neurotoxicity; avoid in renal failure; MAOI interaction
Codeine 10% patients cannot metabolize; unreliable analgesia; neonatal death risk in breastfeeding
Tramadol Serotonin syndrome risk; seizure risk; CNS side effects common

Equipotent Doses (IV)#

  • Morphine 10mg = Hydromorphone 1.5mg = Fentanyl 0.1mg = Meperidine 75mg

Opioid Agonist-Antagonists#

Drug Dose Notes
Buprenorphine 0.3 mg IV/IM q6h Ceiling effect on respiratory depression
Nalbuphine 10-20 mg IV/IM q3-6h
Butorphanol 0.5-2 mg IV q3-4h

非鴉片類止痛藥 (Nonopioid Analgesics)#

Drug Dose Max Daily Notes
Acetaminophen 325-1000 mg PO q4-6h 3-4 g/day No platelet effect; hepatotoxic >4g
1 g IV q6h (>50kg) 4 g/day
Aspirin 325-650 mg PO q4h 4 g/day Platelet dysfunction; GI bleeding; tinnitus = toxicity
Ibuprofen 400-800 mg PO q4-6h 2.4 g/day
Naproxen 250-500 mg PO q8-12h 1.25 g/day
Ketorolac 30 mg IV/IM q6h 120 mg/day (60mg if >65y or <50kg) GI bleeding risk > ibuprofen; limit 3d IV, 5d PO
Ketamine 0.15-0.4 mg/kg IV over 10 min - Low-dose subdissociative; opioid-sparing; hallucinations at higher doses

局部止痛 (Topical Analgesics)#

Agent Preparation Application
Diclofenac gel 1% 4g for knees/ankles, 2g for elbows/wrists; max 32g/day
Lidocaine 5% gel/patch Apply to painful area; max 12h/day
Capsaicin 0.025-0.075% cream Apply 3-4x/day; 8% patch for postherpetic neuralgia (60 min, professional)

止吐 Adjuncts#

Drug Dose Notes
Prochlorperazine 5-10 mg IV/IM EPS risk
Promethazine 25-50 mg IV/IM EPS risk
Metoclopramide 5-10 mg IV/IM EPS risk

給藥途徑#

Route Advantages Disadvantages
PO Easy, cheap, painless Slow onset, GI absorption variable
IV Rapid onset, titratable Venous access needed
IM/SC No IV needed Painful, variable absorption
Transdermal Painless Slow onset, not for acute pain
Inhalational (N2O) Rapid onset/offset Requires cooperation, scavenging

特殊考量#

  • Head injury：可能限制止痛藥以執行神經學檢查
  • Cardiac ischemia：目標是消除所有疼痛
  • Transdermal fentanyl/buprenorphine：慢性疼痛用；急性疼痛治療時移除貼片
  • NSAIDs：增加心血管死亡風險 (COX-2 selective > nonselective)；急性腎衰竭風險族群：老年人、脫水、腎/心疾病、loop diuretics
