# Anatomy and Physiology

This chapter covers the structure and function of the skin.

---

## Structure and Function of the Skin

### Functions of the Skin

The skin is a large, complex organ with a variety of important functions:

- Mechanical barrier.
- Temperature regulation.
- Fluid balance.
- Immune response.

### Layers of the Skin

The skin has three layers:

1. **Epidermis.**
2. **Dermis.**
3. **Hypodermis** (adipose tissue, sweat glands, blood vessels, and lymphatics).

### Layers of the Epidermis

From surface to base:

1. **Stratum corneum** (keratin).
2. **Stratum lucidum.**
3. **Stratum granulosum.**
4. **Stratum spinosum** (desmosomes).
5. **Stratum basale.**
