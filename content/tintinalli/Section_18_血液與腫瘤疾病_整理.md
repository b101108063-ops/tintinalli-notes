# Section 18：血液與腫瘤疾病 (Hematologic and Oncologic Disorders)

---

## Anemia 分類

| 機制 | 範例 |
|------|------|
| **血液流失** | Acute GI bleeding、trauma |
| **破壞增加** | Sickle cell disease、autoimmune hemolytic anemia |
| **製造減少** | Iron/B12/folate deficiency、aplastic anemia |
| **稀釋性** | Rapid IV crystalloid infusion |

---

## Anemia 診斷

### 正常值 (成人)

| 項目 | 男性 | 女性 |
|------|------|------|
| **Hemoglobin** | 14-17 g/dL | 12-15 g/dL |
| **Hematocrit** | 42-52% | 36-48% |
| **MCV** | 78-100 fL | 78-102 fL |
| **Reticulocytes** | 0.5-2.5% | 0.5-2.5% |

### MCV 分類

| 類型 | MCV | 病因 |
|------|-----|------|
| **Microcytic** | <80 fL | Iron deficiency、thalassemia、anemia of chronic disease、lead poisoning |
| **Normocytic** | 80-100 fL | Anemia of chronic disease、renal insufficiency、hemolysis |
| **Macrocytic** | >100 fL | B12/folate deficiency、alcohol abuse、liver disease、myelodysplastic |

### 實驗室檢查

| 檢查 | 意義 |
|------|------|
| **MCV** | RBC 大小 |
| **RDW** | RBC 大小變異性（早期缺乏時可先上升）|
| **Ferritin** | 鐵儲存量（low = iron deficiency）|
| **Reticulocyte count** | 骨髓製造能力（low = 製造減少；high = 破壞或流失）|
| **Direct Coombs test** | 自體免疫溶血性貧血 |
| **Peripheral smear** | RBC morphology、abnormal shapes |

---

## Hemolytic Anemia

### 病因

| 類型 | 病因 |
|------|------|
| **Intrinsic** | G6PD deficiency、sickle cell disease、hereditary spherocytosis |
| **Extrinsic** | Autoimmune、AIHA、microangiopathic (TTP/HUS/DIC)、infection |

### 臨床表現

- Jaundice、splenomegaly、dark urine (hemoglobinuria)
- Elevated indirect bilirubin、LDH、low haptoglobin

---

## Sickle Cell Disease

### 臨床表現

| 併發症 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| **Vaso-occlusive crisis** | Pain crisis（最常見）、dactylitis |
| **Acute chest syndrome** | New pulmonary infiltrate + chest pain/fever/respiratory symptoms |
| **Splenic sequestration** | Acute splenic enlargement、severe anemia（可致命）|
| **Aplastic crisis** | Parvovirus B19 infection → reticulocytopenia |
| **Stroke** | Ischemic 或 hemorrhagic（兒童和成人）|
| **Priapism** | 需要 urology consultation |

### 治療

| 治療 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| **Pain management** | Opioids（不要只用 NSAIDs）|
| **Hydration** | NS 或 D5 1/2NS |
| **Exchange transfusion** | 用於 stroke、acute chest syndrome |
| **Hydroxyurea** | 長期治療（增加 HbF）|
| **Voxelotor** | HbS polymerization inhibitor |

### Stroke 篩檢

- **Transcranial Doppler (TCD)**：適用於 2-16 歲兒童
- Vmax >200 cm/s → 開始 exchange transfusion

---

## Coagulation Tests

| 檢查 | 測量 | 異常見於 |
|------|------|----------|
| **PT** | Extrinsic pathway (Factor VII) | Liver disease、warfarin、Vitamin K deficiency |
| **aPTT** | Intrinsic pathway (VIII, IX, XI, XII) | Heparin、lupus anticoagulant |
| **TT** | Thrombin → fibrinogen | Heparin、DIC、hypofibrinogenemia |
| **Fibrinogen** | Clotting factor I | DIC（low）、inflammation（high）|
| **D-dimer** | Fibrin degradation | DIC、PE/DVT、VTE |

---

## Disseminated Intravascular Coagulation (DIC)

### 病因

- **Sepsis**（最常見）
- Trauma、burns、obstetric complications (amniotic fluid embolus、placental abruption)
- Malignancy、severe transfusion reactions

### 診斷

| 實驗室 | 發現 |
|--------|------|
| **Platelets** | Low |
| **PT/aPTT** | Prolonged |
| **Fibrinogen** | Low |
| **D-dimer** | High |
| **Peripheral smear** | Schistocytes |

### 治療

| 治療 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| **Treat underlying cause** | 最重要 |
| **支持性输血** | Platelets、FFP、cryoprecipitate |
| **Heparin** | 只用於血栓為主且無出血時 |

---

## Thrombocytopenia

### 病因分類

| 類型 | 機制 | 病因 |
|------|------|------|
| **Decreased production** | 骨髓問題 | Aplastic anemia、myelodysplastic、chemotherapy |
| **Increased destruction** | Immune | ITP、TTP/HUS、DIC、heparin-induced |
| **Sequestration** | 脾臟滯留 | Hypersplenism |
| **Dilutional** | 輸血 | Massive transfusion |

### Heparin-Induced Thrombocytopenia (HIT)

| 特點 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| **Timing** | 術後 5-14 天（type I）或 >14 天（type II）|
| **4 T's score** | Thrombocytopenia、Timing、Thrombosis、other causes |
| **抗 PF4/heparin Ab** | Positive |
| **治療** | **停用所有 heparin**、用 argatroban 或 bivalirudin |

### Immune Thrombocytopenia (ITP)

| 特點 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| **診斷標準** | Isolated thrombocytopenia、no other causes |
| **骨髓檢查** | 不需要（典型 ITP 骨髓正常）|

**治療：**
| 情況 | 治療 |
|------|------|
| **無出血** | Monitor（除非 PLT <20-30K）|
| **有出血** | Steroids、IVIG |
| **緊急** | Platelet transfusion + methylprednisolone 1g IV |
| **慢性/復發** | Splenectomy、rituximab、thrombopoietin receptor agonists |

---

## Thrombocytosis

| 病因 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| **Primary** | Myeloproliferative neoplasms（ET、PV、CML）|
| **Secondary** | Infection、inflammation、iron deficiency、malignancy |

**症狀性治療：** Low-dose aspirin（預防血栓）

---

## Venous Thromboembolism (VTE)

### Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT)

**風險因素：**
- Virchow's triad：stasis、endothelial injury、hypercoagulability
- 手術、創傷、cancer、pregnancy、OCP、immobility

**診斷：**
| 檢查 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| **Wells DVT score** | Clinical probability |
| **Compression US** | Gold standard |
| **D-dimer** | 排除 VTE（如果 low probability）|

### Pulmonary Embolism (PE)

**臨床表現：**
- Dyspnea（最常見）、pleuritic chest pain、hemoptysis
- Hypoxia、tachycardia、tachypnea

**診斷：**
| 檢查 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| **CT pulmonary angiography** | Gold standard |
| **V/Q scan** | 如果 CT contraindicated |
| **ECG** | Sinus tachycardia、S1Q3T3（右心 strain）|
| **Echo** | 右心擴大、dysfunction（大量 PE）|

**嚴重程度：**
| 指標 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| **Massive PE** | Hypotension、shock |
| **Submassive PE** | Normal BP + RV dysfunction |
| **Low-risk PE** | Normal BP + no RV dysfunction |

### VTE 治療

| 治療 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| **Anticoagulation** | DOAC（apixaban、rivaroxaban、dabigatran、edoxaban）或 LMWH |
| **Parenteral anticoag** | Heparin 或 LMWH（橋接warfarin）|
| **Warfarin** | Target INR 2-3 |
| **Thrombolysis** | Massive PE（systolic BP <90）、大量 PE 48h 內 |
| **Catheter-directed therapy** | 無法做 thrombolysis 的 massive PE |
| **IVC filter** | Contraindication to anticoagulation |

### Thrombolysis 禁忌症

| 絕對 | 相對 |
|------|------|
| Active bleeding | Recent surgery >3 days |
| Intracranial hemorrhage | Ischemic stroke <3 months |
| Brain tumor | GI bleeding <3 months |
| Aortic dissection | Platelet <100K |
| | SBP >180 或 DBP >110 |

---

## Anticoagulant Reversal

| 藥物 | Reversal |
|------|----------|
| **Warfarin** | Vitamin K 5-10 mg IV + FFP 或 PCC |
| **Heparin (UFH)** | Protamine 1 mg per 100U（max 50 mg）|
| **LMWH** | Protamine 1 mg per 100 anti-Xa units |
| **Fondaparinux** | Protamine（部分逆轉）|
| **DOAC (apixaban/rivaroxaban)** | Andexanet alfa（if major bleeding）|
| **Dabigatran** | Idarucizumab（Praxbind）|
| **Thrombolytics** | Cryoprecipitate、aminocaproic acid |

---

## Bleeding Disorders

### Hemophilia A/B

| 類型 | 缺陷 | 治療 |
|------|------|------|
| **Hemophilia A** | Factor VIII deficiency | DDAVP 或 recombinant VIII |
| **Hemophilia B** | Factor IX deficiency | Recombinant IX |

**臨床表現：** Hemarthrosis（關節出血）、muscle hematoma、epistaxis

### von Willebrand Disease

| 類型 | 缺陷 | 治療 |
|------|------|------|
| **vWD type 1** | Partial quantitative deficiency | DDAVP |
| **vWD type 2** | Qualitative defect | Factor VIII concentrate |
| **vWD type 3** | Complete deficiency | Factor VIII concentrate |

### 出血治療原則

| 情況 | 治療 |
|------|------|
| **Hemophilia A/B** | Replace missing factor |
| **vWD** | DDAVP 或 factor concentrate |
| **Platelet disorder** | Platelet transfusion |
| **Fibrinogen deficiency** | Cryoprecipitate |

---

## Transfusion Medicine

### Blood Products

| 製品 | 適應症 |
|------|--------|
| **Packed RBC** | Anemia、blood loss |
| **Platelet** | Thrombocytopenia、bleeding |
| **FFP** | Coagulation factor deficiency、warfarin reversal |
| **Cryoprecipitate** | Hypofibrinogenemia、DIC |
| **PCC** | Warfarin reversal、major bleeding |

### Transfusion Reactions

| 類型 | 表現 | 治療 |
|------|------|------|
| **Febrile non-hemolytic** | Fever、chills | Antipyretics |
| **Allergic** | Urticaria、pruritus | Antihistamines、epinephrine（嚴重時）|
| **Acute hemolytic** | Fever、pain、dark urine、hypotension | **立即停止**、fluid、supportive |
| **TRALI** | Respiratory distress、hypoxia | **立即停止**、oxygen、supportive |
| **TACO** | Volume overload、dyspnea | Diuretics |
| **Septic** | 高燒、hypotension | **立即停止**、antibiotics |

## Oncologic Emergencies

### Febrile Neutropenia

| 定義 | ANC <500 或 <1000 且預期會下降 + fever >38.3°C |
|------|--------------------------------------|

**治療：**
- **Broad-spectrum antibiotics**：Pip/Tazo 或 cefepime 或 meropenem
- **Pseudomonas coverage** 很重要

### Superior Vena Cava Syndrome

| 病因 | Lung cancer（最常見）、lymphoma |
|------|--------------------------------|

**表現：** Facial swelling、plethora、JVD、HA、visual changes

**治療：**
- **Steroids**：Dexamethasone 4-10 mg q6h
- **Radiation/chemo**：取決於腫瘤類型
- **支架**：有呼吸窘迫時

### Spinal Cord Compression

| 病因 | Metastatic cancer（breast、lung、prostate）|
|------|--------------------------------------|

**表現：** Back pain、leg weakness、sensory level、bladder dysfunction

**診斷：** MRI（gold standard）

**治療：**
- **Dexamethasone** 10 mg IV → 4 mg q6h
- **Radiation therapy**
- **Surgery**：如果椎體不穩定

### Tumor Lysis Syndrome

| 病因 | Rapid cell death（lymphoma、leukemia）|
|------|----------------------------------|

**實驗室：** Hyperuricemia、hyperkalemia、hyperphosphatemia、hypocalcemia

**治療：**
- **Hydration**
- **Allopurinol** 或 **rasburicase**
- **Treat hyperkalemia**

### Hypercalcemia of Malignancy

| 病因 | PTHrP、bone metastases |
|------|------------------------|

**治療：**
- **Hydration**（NS 200-500 mL/h）
- **Bisphosphonates**：Zoledronic acid 或 pamidronate
- **Calcitonin**：快速但不持久
- **Hemodialysis**：如果 renal failure

### SIADH

| 病因 | Small cell lung cancer（中樞神經系統疾病、藥物）|
|------|------------------------------------------|

**實驗室：** Low serum osmolality、low serum sodium、high urine osmolality

**治療：**
- **Fluid restriction**
- **Hypertonic saline**：如果嚴重 symptomatic hyponatremia
- **Vaptans**：Vasopressin receptor antagonists

## Myeloproliferative Neoplasms

| 疾病 | 特徵 | 急性轉化 |
|------|------|----------|
| **Polycythemia vera** | Elevated RBC、low EPO | AML、MDS |
| **Essential thrombocythemia** | Elevated platelets | AML、MDS |
| **Primary myelofibrosis** | Bone marrow fibrosis | AML |
| **CML** | Elevated WBC、Philadelphia chromosome | AML |

---

## Leukemia

### Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (ALL)

| 特徵 | 兒童最常見（peak 2-5歲）|
|------|------------------------|

**表現：** Fatigue、bleeding、infections、bone pain、lymphadenopathy

### Acute Myeloid Leukemia (AML)

| 特徵 | 成人最常見的 acute leukemia |
|------|--------------------------|

**表現：** Similar to ALL、often presents in older adults

### Chronic Myeloid Leukemia (CML)

| 特徵 | Philadelphia chromosome、BCR-ABL |
|------|---------------------------------|

**治療：** Imatinib（Gleevec）- tyrosine kinase inhibitor

---

## Lymphoma

### Hodgkin Lymphoma

| 特徵 | Reed-Sternberg cells |
|------|----------------------|

**表現：** Painless lymphadenopathy、B symptoms

### Non-Hodgkin Lymphoma

| 特徵 | 多種亞型、more disseminated |
|------|---------------------------|

**侵襲性亞型：** 需要化療

---

## Multiple Myeloma

| 特徵 | Plasma cell malignancy |
|------|-----------------------|

**症狀：** "CRAB" criteria
- **C**：Hypercalcemia
- **R**：Renal insufficiency
- **A**：Anemia
- **B**：Bone lesions

**診斷：** SPEP（M spike）、urine protein、Bone marrow biopsy

**治療：**
- **Transplant-eligible**：Bortezomib + lenalidomide + dexamethasone
- **Transplant-ineligible**：Lenalidomide + dexamethasone
